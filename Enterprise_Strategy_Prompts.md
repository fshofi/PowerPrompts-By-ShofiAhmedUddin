# Enterprise Strategy Power Prompts
_Run with your numbers. Export output to a sheet. Validate with finance before action._

---

## 1) Audit Profit Leakage
**Role:** CFO  
Audit [BUSINESS] to locate profit leaks across cost, pricing, and operations. 
Focus on the five highest-ROI opportunities. 
Context: [INDUSTRY], revenue [AMOUNT].  
**Output:** Problem / Fix / Estimated Financial Impact.  
End with **Highest-ROI Move** (two lines).

---

## 2) Diagnose Market Positioning
**Role:** Brand strategist  
Analyze [BUSINESS] for positioning and message clarity. 
Flag generic, unclear, or misaligned elements.  
**Output (table):** Issue / Why it matters / Fix.  
Add a one-paragraph differentiation summary.

---

## 3) Simulate Pricing Power
**Role:** Pricing consultant  
Assess current pricing. Recommend three strategies to raise profit without new products or churn.  
Context: [INDUSTRY], high retention, flat margins.  
**Output (table):** Approach / Risk / Expected Upside.  
Add **Why the middle option wins** (≈100 words).

---

## 4) Check Leadership Alignment
**Role:** Founder coach  
Create a five-question diagnostic for leadership alignment in [BUSINESS] 
(decision-making, accountability, execution).  
**Output (table):** Question / Why it matters.  
Add a 1–5 scoring guide and one “if score ≤3 then…” action.

---

## 5) Assess Investor Readiness
**Role:** VC associate  
Score Team, Market, Traction, and Scalability (1–10). 
For each: one strength and one weakness.  
**Output (table):** Area / Score / Strength / Weakness / Next Move.  
Finish with **Funding Readiness Outlook** (five lines).

---

## 6) Scan Growth Bottlenecks
**Role:** COO  
Identify the three primary constraints in [BUSINESS] across people, process, or product.  
**Output:** Bottleneck / Why it matters / Fix.  
Add **Compounding effect if unresolved** (bullet list).

---

## 7) Uplift Retention Strategy
**Role:** Retention strategist  
Propose three experiments to increase LTV by 20% in 90 days without ads or discounts.  
**Output:** Idea / Execution / Expected Impact.  
End with **Most feasible experiment + rationale**.

---

## 8) Reallocate Founder Time
**Role:** Executive performance coach  
Audit founder [NAME] time across strategy, management, operations.  
**Output (table):** Focus Area / Recommendation / Expected Time Gain.  
Conclude with **Highest leverage shifts** (bullets).
