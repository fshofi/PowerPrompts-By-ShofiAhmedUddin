# ChatGPT Mastery Prompts
_Designed to demonstrate clarity, constraint, reflection, and iteration._

---

## 1) Clarity Compass
**Purpose:** Turn vague asks into precise tasks.  
**Prompt:**  
Before answering, restate my request in its clearest, most specific form. 
List any ambiguities you see. Rewrite the question to remove them. 
Answer the revised version only.

---

## 2) Friction Finder
**Purpose:** Surface the real blocker.  
**Prompt:**  
Ask me three short questions to locate the core friction behind [GOAL]. 
Name the friction in one line, then propose the smallest step that removes it today.

---

## 3) Timeboxed Perspective
**Purpose:** Focus depth with time.  
**Prompt:**  
Give a 120-second read guide to [TOPIC]: three most important ideas, one pitfall, one action.

---

## 4) Decision Lens
**Purpose:** Force trade-offs to decide with intent.  
**Prompt:**  
List three approaches to [CHALLENGE]. For each: **Gain / Cost / Risk**. 
Choose one and justify in under 100 words. State the success metric.

---

## 5) Persona Lock
**Purpose:** Keep voice and role consistent.  
**Prompt:**  
Speak as [ROLE, e.g., “technical editor for executives”]. 
Tone: [TONE]. Audience: [AUDIENCE]. Objective: [OUTCOME]. Confirm the brief, then deliver.

---

## 6) Collaboration Loop
**Purpose:** Draft → critique → rewrite.  
**Prompt:**  
Draft [ASSET]. Then critique your draft with bullets on tone, structure, and logic. 
Rewrite once using your critique. Show the key changes.

---

## 7) Tone Transformer
**Purpose:** Control emotional color without losing content.  
**Prompt:**  
Rewrite this text in three tones: **neutral / warm / executive**. 
Preserve facts. Output as a table: *Tone / Revised Text / When to use*.

---

## 8) Negative Space Prompt
**Purpose:** Say what to avoid.  
**Prompt:**  
Answer the question, but **exclude**: [LIST OF OFF-TOPICS, JARGON, OR BANNED CLAIMS]. 
If an exclusion is necessary for accuracy, flag it and propose a safe alternative.

---

## 9) Reflective Mirror
**Purpose:** Self-critique for quality.  
**Prompt:**  
Review your previous answer like a peer reviewer. 
Identify one assumption, one missing angle, and one weak chain of reasoning. 
Revise only those parts.

---

## 10) System Refiner
**Purpose:** Turn insight into a reusable template.  
**Prompt:**  
Convert our conversation into a framework with steps. 
Output table: *Step / Description / Value / Inputs Needed*. 
Add a 3-line “How to reuse” note.
